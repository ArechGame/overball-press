About Overball

Overball is a fast-paced local multiplayer party game inspired by Pong, where strategy quickly gives way to pure chaos.

Defend your goal, destroy your opponents' defenses and survive increasingly intense rallies as the ball accelerates with every hit. Collect powerful upgrades, adapt to unexpected events and outplay your friends in frantic arcade matches.

Play with up to six players locally, fill teams with AI opponents, customize match rules and discover multiple game modes, each offering its own twist on the classic formula.

Key Features

🧱 Destructible Defenses

Break through enemy walls to open new scoring opportunities and completely change the battlefield.

⚡ Powerful Upgrades

Collect upgrades that transform the match and create unexpected, chaotic situations.

👥 Up to 6 Players

Play locally with up to six players or add AI teammates and opponents to complete the teams.

🎯 Three Game Modes

Play classic Versus matches, intense 1v1 Duels or the unique Dodge Ball mode with moving goals.

🎲 Custom Match Rules

Adjust match duration, event frequency and bonus spawn rate to create your own style of play.

💥 Pure Arcade Chaos

Every hit makes the ball faster, forcing players to react quicker as matches become increasingly frantic.
