About Overball

Overball is a fast-paced local multiplayer party game inspired by Pong, where strategy quickly gives way to pure chaos.

Defend your goal, destroy your opponents' defenses and survive increasingly intense rallies as the ball accelerates with every hit. Collect powerful upgrades, adapt to unexpected events and outplay your friends in frantic arcade matches.

Play with up to six players locally, fill teams with AI opponents, customize match rules and discover multiple game modes, each offering its own twist on the classic formula.

Why You'll Love Overball

Simple to understand, difficult to master. Every match quickly turns into fast-paced chaos thanks to destructible defenses, unpredictable power-ups and endless customization.
